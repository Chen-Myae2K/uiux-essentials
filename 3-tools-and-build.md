## ğŸ§© User-Centered Design (UCD)

**Definition:**

User-Centered Design is a design approach that focuses on the needs, preferences, and limitations of the end users throughout the entire product development process. The goal is to create products that are **usable, efficient, and satisfying** for the people who will use them.
