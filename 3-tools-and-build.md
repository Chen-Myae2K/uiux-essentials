### **1. Human Factors in UX (Short)**

Human factors are the things that make a product easy and comfortable for real people to use.

### **Key Points:**

- **Cognitive load:** Keep tasks simple.
- **Accessibility:** Design for everyone.
- **Emotion & trust:** Make the experience feel safe and friendly.
- **Usability:** Easy navigation and clear actions.
- **Human behavior:** Users scan, make mistakes, prefer familiar patterns.
- **Physical factors:** Comfortable button sizes and interactions.

### **2. Mental Model**

### **3. Positive Feedback Loop**

A **positive feedback loop** makes an action **increase** or **grow** over time.

- The room becomes brighter

    â¡ï¸ **Result: Light increases (action and result go in the same direction).**
    

### **4. Negative Feedback Loop**
