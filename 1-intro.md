## üë©‚Äçüé® Type of Designers

### 1. Generalist Designer

**What It Is:** Broad skills across UI, UX, branding, illustration, content

**Benefit:** Flexible, valuable for small teams or fast-moving projects

**How the Role Works:** Switch between different design tasks based on project demands

### 2. Specialist Designer

**What It Is:** Focuses deeply on one discipline (e.g., UX research, UI design)

**Benefit:** Expert-level quality in specialty, ideal for complex work

**How the Role Works:** Concentrates on tasks within specialty, collaborates with others

### 3. T-Shaped Designer

**What It Is:** Broad knowledge plus deep expertise in one core area

**Benefit:** Balances flexibility and craft, collaborates broadly while leading specialty

**How the Role Works:** Contributes across workflow, leads on specialty area
